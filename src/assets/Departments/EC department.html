Overview

Established in 2004 with an intake of 120 the department has always remained on the forefront in producing quality engineers in the field of Electronics & Communication, who have brought great laurels to the institute. The branch has a tradition of attracting the topmost merit students at UG from across the state. We offer excellent infrastructure and resources in the form of knowledgeable faculty, well equipped laboratories, library having a vast collection of informative books and journals. The department has got well equipped hardware and software laboratories as per the R.G.P.V. (university of technology of Madhya Pradesh) norms where students perform various experiments and projects related to the subjects. The major laboratories in the department are Analog & Digital Communication, Antenna and Wave Propagation, Signal Processing, Microwave& Optical Communication, TV & RADAR, Network & Transmission lines, Devices and Circuits, Embedded Systems, Digital Electronics. Faculty members are working in research areas related to cutting edge technologies such as VLSI and Embedded Systems, Communication, Signal Processing, Optical Communication, Wireless Communication. We offer industry institute interaction through industrial visits & expert lectures. Moreover, the department is involved in a number of technical and co-curricular activities encouraging students to broaden their horizons of thought, innovate and implement their ideas.
